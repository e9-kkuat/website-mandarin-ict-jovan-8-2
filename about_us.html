关于金汉堡包饭店 | About Golden Hamburger Restaurant

各位顾客大家好，我是金汉堡包饭店的老板 Jovan Manuel。 我喜欢吃西餐比如薯条、汉堡包和炸鸡，但我不喜欢中餐。我打开金汉堡包是为了欢迎光临西餐走进中国人，感受西餐的正宗味道。你可以吃西餐，比如汉堡包、薯条或炸鸡。饮料方面还有可乐、冰茶等等。

Hello my fellow customers, I am the owner of the Golden Hamburger, Jovan Manuel. I personally really like to eat the western foods like fries, hamburger, and fried chicken, but I dislike the Chinese foods. I opened this restaurant to welcome the western style foods into the Chinese people, to feel the authentic taste of the western foods. You can eat western foods like hamburger, fries, or fried chicken. Also for beverages, we have coke, iced tea, and many more.
